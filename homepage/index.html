The Journey So Far

See the Straw Hat Crew's journey through the Grand Line!

East Blue

After meeting the Red Hair Pirates, eating the Gum-Gum Fruit, and becoming determined to be the next Pirate King, Luffy sets sail and experiences his first adventures while recruiting the first members of the Straw Hat Pirates.

Paradise

Luffy and his crewmates embark on a thrilling and perilous adventure, each with their own goal. In their journey they encounter various islands and formidable foes. Setting sail with no destination, their ultimate goal is to find the legendary One Piece.

New World

Following the events of Marineford and Luffy and his crew reconvene at Sabaody Archipelago. Determined to find the One Piece and continue their journey, the crew sets off into the New World, otherwise known as the Pirate's Graveyard.
